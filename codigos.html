Codigos - unwriten rules of the milonga

  1. Follow the line of the dance: counter-clockwise keeping close to the walls.
  2. Invitation to the dance is performed only by the cabaseo (even if you are sitting at the same table)
  3. NO BACK steps. it is possible to do back steps only direction to the centre.
  4. If you come as a couple, the first tanda you should dance with each other. Up to this point, you are closed for invitations.
  5. If a girl is sitting next to her partner, and you want to ask her for a dance, you should ask permission of her partner.
  6. If the couple who came together after the first tanda sits at different tables, they open up each other to other people's invitations without any permissions.
  7. Responsibility for the safety of the couple during the dance is a man.
  8. If you have touched another couple or another couple touched you, gentleman should apologise to their partners.
  9. With heavy traffic in the milonga man should not lead any high boles, and follower should not do any high decorations that may offend other couples.
  10. DO NOT CROSS THE DANCE FLOOR.. No matter you are dancing now or not.
  11. During the Cortina dance floor should be empty.
  12. Invitation lasts only ONE TANDA. DO NOT DANCE MORE THEN 2 tandas with a same partner during milonga! MORE then 2 pandas only with your wife/husband.
  13. Dance La Cumparsita with your partner or husband/wife. Dansing La Cumparsita with a stranger is a proposal to continue this night together. If you dance last tanda with a stranger, it is polite to separate and take your seat when La Cumparsita stats.
  14. Choose what kind of music, and with whom to dance or not to dance.
  15. To invite only the last song in a tanda is not polite.
  16. Loud talking and laughter can be distracting for dancers, RESPECT other people's comfort!!!
  17. Milonga is a resting place, it is not a class, DO NOT TEACH during the milonga. Dance and be happy!
